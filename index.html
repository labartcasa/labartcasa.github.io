LabartCasa Robotics Smart Storage Furniture

Store it once. Ask for it later.

LabartCasa is a patent-pending smart storage furniture that learns what you put inside and retrieves the right item when you ask.

Say “Where are my keys?” or “Give me something sharp,” and the system locates items automatically.

How it works

Place items inside. The system learns each item using sensors and recognition. When you need something, just ask, and it gives you what you asked for.

The goal is simple: less searching, fewer “where did I put it?” moments, and faster daily routines.

Use cases

  • Grab your keys and wallet on the way out.
  • In the kitchen: “Give me something sharp.”
  • Find the right cable without digging through a drawer.
  • Quick access to glasses, chargers, small tools, or meds (where appropriate).
